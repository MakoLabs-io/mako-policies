Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 11, 2025

Mako Technology Labs LLC (“Mako,” “we,” “us”) publishes mobile and web apps including Smolov Squat Tracker. This Privacy Policy explains how this app handles information.

What We Collect

No personal data is collected, stored, or sold. Smolov Squat Tracker runs entirely on your device. Workout data such as your squat weights, sets, and progress are saved locally on your phone or tablet and never transmitted to us or any third party.

Analytics & Third Parties

We do not include advertising, and we do not share any personal information with third parties. If we later enable optional, anonymous analytics (for example, crash reporting or basic usage metrics), we will update this policy and identify the provider and data types collected.

Data Security

All data remains stored locally on your device. The app relies on the standard security protections provided by iOS and Android. We do not operate servers or maintain user accounts for Smolov Squat Tracker.

Health and Fitness Data

Smolov Squat Tracker does not access or write data to Apple HealthKit, Google Fit, or any health-related API at this time. If such integrations are added in the future, we will request your explicit consent and update this policy accordingly.

Children’s Privacy

Smolov Squat Tracker is designed for general fitness use by adults and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. Any material updates will be posted within the app and include a new “Last updated” date.
