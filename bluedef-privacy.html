Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 3, 2025

Mako Technology Labs LLC (“Mako,” “we,” “us”) publishes mobile and web apps including Blue Def Decoder. This Privacy Policy explains how our apps handle information.

What We Collect

No personal data is collected, stored, or sold. Blue Def Decoder operates on-device. The code you enter is processed locally solely to display a manufacturing date and freshness status.

Analytics & Third Parties

We do not embed advertising and we do not share personal data with third parties. If we enable anonymous, aggregate analytics in the future (e.g., crash reports, basic usage), we will update this policy and disclose the provider and data types.

Data Security

Because we do not collect personal data, we don’t maintain user accounts or profiles. Standard platform safeguards (iOS/Android) apply to the app binary itself.

Children’s Privacy

Our apps do not target children and do not collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this page to reflect changes in our practices. Material changes will include a new “Last updated” date.
