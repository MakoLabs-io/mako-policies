Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Mako Technology Labs LLC ("Mako," "we," "us") publishes mobile and web apps including BiteSignal. This Privacy Policy explains how this app handles information.

Information We Collect

BiteSignal stores your fishing data — trips, catches, spots, and preferences — locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to Mako Technology Labs LLC or stored on our servers.

To provide real-time fishing conditions, the app accesses the following data:

  • Device location: Your GPS coordinates are used to fetch local tide predictions, weather conditions, and solunar data. Location is sent to third-party services (listed below) to retrieve this data and is not stored by Mako Technology Labs LLC.
  • Purchase information: If you subscribe to BiteSignal Pro, your subscription is managed by Apple and RevenueCat. We do not collect your payment details. RevenueCat receives an anonymous device identifier to manage your subscription status.

Third-Party Services

BiteSignal uses the following third-party services to provide core functionality:

  • NOAA Tides & Currents — Tide predictions and tidal station data. Public domain, operated by the U.S. National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration. tidesandcurrents.noaa.gov
  • Open-Meteo — Weather data including wind, pressure, temperature, cloud cover, and precipitation. Licensed under CC BY 4.0. open-meteo.com
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management. Receives an anonymous identifier and purchase receipts to validate your subscription status. revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Apple Maps / Google Maps — Map display within the app, provided by the respective platform SDKs.

We do not share or sell user data. BiteSignal does not include advertising, advertising identifiers, or third-party tracking technologies.

Local Storage of Fishing Data

All fishing data you enter into BiteSignal — including trips, catches, spots, species, baits, notes, photos, and preferences — is stored locally on your device using an on-device database. This data exists solely for your personal use and is not uploaded to external servers or cloud services operated by us.

Location Data

BiteSignal requests access to your device's location to provide accurate fishing conditions for your area. Location data is:

  • Used only while the app is open (foreground location access)
  • Sent to NOAA and Open-Meteo to retrieve tide and weather data for your coordinates
  • Not stored on our servers or shared with data brokers
  • Not used for advertising or tracking purposes

You may also set a location manually within the app without granting GPS access.

Subscription and Payment Data

BiteSignal Pro subscriptions are processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment card number, bank account, or Apple ID credentials. Subscription status is verified through RevenueCat using anonymous device identifiers.

Data Security

All fishing information stored by BiteSignal remains on your device. The app relies on the security protections provided by the operating system of your device (such as iOS). Mako Technology Labs LLC does not operate servers or maintain user accounts for this application.

Data Retention and Deletion

All fishing data in BiteSignal is stored locally on your device and is retained only as long as the app remains installed.

You have full control over your data and may delete it at any time by:

  • Using the "Reset all data" option in the app's Settings screen
  • Deleting individual trips, catches, or spots within the app
  • Clearing the app's storage through your device settings
  • Uninstalling the application, which permanently removes all locally stored data

Because Mako Technology Labs LLC does not collect or store user data on external servers, we cannot access, retrieve, or delete your data on your behalf.

Children's Privacy

BiteSignal is intended for general use and is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices, features, or legal requirements. When updates are made, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised.
