Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Mako Technology Labs LLC (“Mako,” “we,” “us”) publishes mobile and web applications including RollJournal Timer. This Privacy Policy explains how RollJournal Timer handles information.

Overview

RollJournal Timer is designed as a training timer for Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, grappling, MMA, boxing, conditioning, and other interval-based workouts. The app operates primarily on the user’s device and does not require the creation of user accounts or the transmission of personal data to servers operated by Mako Technology Labs LLC.

Timer and App Data

RollJournal Timer allows users to create, save, and run custom timers for training sessions. As part of this functionality, the app stores information that users voluntarily enter in order to configure and reuse timers.

This information may include:

  • Saved timer names
  • Round length and number of rounds
  • Rest periods
  • Warning times
  • Start delay settings
  • Favorites and recent timer selections
  • Sound and haptic preferences

This information is used solely to provide the core functionality of the app, which is helping users run and manage training timers.

Timer and app data entered into or generated by the app:

  • Is stored locally on the user's device.
  • Is not transmitted to Mako Technology Labs LLC.
  • Is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties.
  • Is accessible only by the user within the application.

Information We Collect

Mako Technology Labs LLC does not collect, transmit, or store personal information from users of RollJournal Timer.

The application does not require user accounts and does not request personally identifiable information such as name, email address, phone number, or location.

Local Storage of Data

All timer configurations, preferences, and related app data are stored locally on the user’s device. This information exists only to support the functionality of the app.

Mako Technology Labs LLC does not operate servers or databases that store user-generated data for this application.

Third-Party Services

RollJournal Timer currently does not include:

  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics services
  • Third-party tracking technologies

The app may include links or references to RollJournal or other Mako Technology Labs LLC products, but RollJournal Timer itself does not transmit user data to those services.

If optional analytics, crash reporting, or similar tools are introduced in the future to improve the reliability or usability of the application, this Privacy Policy will be updated to disclose what data is collected and how it is used.

Health Platform Integrations

RollJournal Timer does not currently access Apple HealthKit, Google Fit, or other health platform APIs.

If such integrations are added in the future, the app will request explicit permission from the user before accessing those services and this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Data Security

Because data is stored locally on the device, its protection relies on the security features provided by the device’s operating system, such as iOS or Android.

Mako Technology Labs LLC does not maintain servers containing user timer or training data for this application.

Data Retention and Deletion

All data created within RollJournal Timer remains on the user’s device and is retained only as long as the application remains installed, unless the user deletes it sooner.

You may delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting saved timers within the app
  • Clearing the application's storage through your device settings
  • Uninstalling the application, which permanently removes all locally stored data

Because we do not collect or store user data externally, we cannot access, retrieve, or delete user data on behalf of users.

Children’s Privacy

RollJournal Timer is intended for a general audience and is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in features, legal requirements, or development practices. When updates occur, the “Last updated” date at the top of this page will be revised.
