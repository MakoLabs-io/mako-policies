Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Mako Technology Labs LLC ("Mako," "we," "us") publishes RollJournal Timer. This Privacy Policy explains how RollJournal Timer handles information.

Overview

RollJournal Timer is a training timer for Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, grappling, MMA, boxing, conditioning, and interval workouts. The app works primarily on the user's device and does not require an account.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store personal information from users of RollJournal Timer.

The app does not require users to provide names, email addresses, phone numbers, location information, or other personal information to use its core features.

Data Stored on Device

RollJournal Timer may store app data locally on the user's device to support core functionality. This may include:

  • Saved timer configurations
  • Timer names and preferences
  • Favorites and recent timer selections
  • Sound and haptic settings

This data is stored locally on the user's device and is not transmitted to Mako Technology Labs LLC.

Third-Party Services

RollJournal Timer does not use advertising networks, third-party analytics, or third-party tracking technologies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

Data Retention and Deletion

Because app data is stored locally on the user's device, users may delete it at any time by deleting saved timers in the app, clearing the app's local storage through device settings, or uninstalling the app.

We cannot access, retrieve, or delete data on behalf of users because we do not receive or store it.

Children's Privacy

RollJournal Timer is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above.
