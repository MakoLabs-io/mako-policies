Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Mako Technology Labs LLC (“Mako,” “we,” “us”) publishes mobile and web apps including Practice Signal. This Privacy Policy explains how this app handles information.

What We Collect

No personal data is collected, stored, or sold by us. Practice Signal runs on your device. Your app data—such as session logs, effort/outcome/intention ratings, focus areas, and reflections—is stored locally on your phone or tablet and is not transmitted to Mako servers.

Analytics & Third Parties

We do not include advertising and do not sell personal information. If we later add optional, anonymous analytics (for example, crash reporting or basic usage metrics), we will update this policy and identify the provider and data types involved.

Data Security

Data remains on your device and is protected by the security features provided by iOS and Android. We do not require user accounts to use Practice Signal.

Health or Wellness Information

Practice Signal supports self-reflection about training and wellness routines. It does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment and does not access Apple HealthKit, Google Fit, or other health platform APIs at this time. If integrations are added in the future, we will request your permission and update this policy.

Children’s Privacy

Practice Signal is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Retention and Deletion

All data created within Practice Signal is stored locally on your device and is retained only as long as the app remains installed on your device.

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting entries directly within the app.
  • Clearing the app's storage through your device settings.
  • Uninstalling the app, which permanently removes all locally stored data.

Because Mako Technology Labs LLC does not collect or store user data on external servers, we cannot access, retrieve, or delete your data on your behalf.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Any material changes will include a revised “Last updated” date.
